Detailed Description

Describes a swarm of physical point-like objects.

Application developer needs to just declare the desired number of SwarmElements and the desired CollectiveCalculators, CollectiveForces, CollectiveInputDetectors, CollectiveRules and CollectivePhenomenons as children of the Swarm.
